Categorical & the many-segment generator

segmented areas

Up to 8 named segments take fixed, ordered hues (a legend). Hundreds of BREP faces can’t be told apart by hue at all — they take a perceptual generator whose only promise is “neighbours differ”, with click-to-identify for the rest.
